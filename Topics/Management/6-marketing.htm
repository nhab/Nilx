Step 1. Collect data and information through observation Step 2. Create tentative descriptions of what is being observed Step 3. Form hypotheses that predict different outcomes based on these observations Step 4. Test your hypotheses Step 5. Analyze the data, drawing conclusions and insights from the results Step 6. Rinse and repeat رشد و بهبود Marketing metrics سنجش پارامترهای بازاریابی 1. CPA – Cost per Acquisition (cost / conversions) 2. CPC – Cost per Click (cost / traffic) 3. CVR – Conversion Rate (conversions / traffic) 4. CTR – Clickthrough Rate (clicks / impressions) 5. CPM – Cost per Mille (cost / impressions * 1000) 6. ROI – Return on Investment (revenue / cost * 100) 7. MSC – Maximum Spend Cap (add up all your campaign budget caps) 8.
