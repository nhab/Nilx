Resume writing 1. Define your career goals

What job,position and company you want?

2. Find job listings from diffrent sources 3. shortlist the companies which suite your criteria

(for example:being mid to big level,technologies they use,their captital and personnel satisfaction)

4. Create a customized resume for each job based on the needed skills in job description:

Your job title: under your name,write 3 skills which are most needed in the job description(of course you must be skiled on them)

Your profiles section: summary of your hard and soft skills and career highlights and your education and certificates

5.Proof of your profile:

Your experience sectionShow your practical experiences by example in the skills they want,company-wised or project-wised.

It is not needed to write mass of skills in skill section,it can instead be shown in your career history,which is referencible.

It is good to refer to a website,people or certificate to prove what you say.

6. Apply massively but Rationally and Research companies you want to apply for 7. Write a tailored cover letter
