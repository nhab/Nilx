Personal brand

    - Is what you widely known for,
      based on your experience, expertise, competencies, actions and/or achievements  within a community, industry, or the marketplace at large.    

    - It is what they think of you.
    
    - It is the value you gave them and they remember you for,
    specialy when they need your service.
    
    - It is as kill. you become better by practicing.
    
    - It is your emotional impact on others.
    
    - Be honest to yourself about your personal brand :
    
       You have not enough reputation among your potential customers,
         not because you are intravert or shy,
         not because of your personality,
         but because we did not give the people a memorable value in our bussiness.
   
    - Ultimately, the goal is to create something that conveys a message and that can be monetized
    
Rules Of Personal Branding

        - Be genuine.
        - Have a focus.
        - Tell a story.
        - Be consistent.
        - Be ready to fail(and learn)
        - Create a positive impact.
        -  Follow a successful example.
        - Live your brand.
        - Let other people tell your story.
        - Leave a legacy.
        - Align your personality(what you intend to show about yourself) with your charachter(How people judge you).
